Lewis Hamilton

Six-Time Formula One World Champion

A timeline of Lewis Hamilton's life:

  • 1985: Born in Stevenage, England
  • 1991: Father buys him his first kart for his 6th birthday
  • 1992: Finishes second in the national championship for radio-controlled cars and makes first TV appearance on Blue Peter
  • 1993: Starts karting
  • 1995: Wins the British Cadet Kart championship
  • 1998: Signs for McLaren Formula One Young Driver Development programme
  • 2000: Wins European Formula A karting championship
  • 2001: Debuts in car racing, competing in the British Formula Renault Winter Series, finishing fifth
  • 2002: Finishes third in Formula Renault UK
  • 2003: Wins Formula Renault UK championship
  • 2004: Finishes fifth in debut Formula Three Euroseries season
  • 2005: Wins Formula Three Euroseries
  • 2006: Wins debut season in GP2
  • 2007: Finishes second in debut season in Formula One
  • 2008: Wins his first Formula One World Championship, aged 23
  • 2009: Finishes fifth in third Formula One season
  • 2010: Finishes fourth in fourth Formula One season
  • 2011: Finishes fifth in fifth Formula One season
  • 2012: Finishes fourth in sixth Formula One season
  • 2013: Joins Mercedes and finishes fourth in first season with his new team
  • 2014: Wins second Formula One World Championship
  • 2015: Wins third Formula One World Championship
  • 2016: Celebrates 10 seasons in Formula One but finishes second to team mate Nico Rosberg
  • 2017: Wins fourth Formula One World Championship
  • 2018: Wins fifth Formula One World Championship
  • 2019: Wins sixth Formula One World Championship
